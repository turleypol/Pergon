In-Game Text Commands

The in-game commands are split up into the following access levels: You can access the commands at your (command) access level and below.

The command levels

  1. Player
  2. Counselor
  3. Questchar
  4. Seer
  5. GM (no extra commands)
  6. (High) GM
  7. GL
  8. Scripter
  9. Test
