Staff can create 0x7033 and with the deed walk around creating trees.
Players can double click on the trees to get their presents.

The trees will decay in 30 days.

To remove all the deeds, just edit the pkg.cfg and disable it.

You can put the item 0x7033 on a merchant and let them sell the trees.
